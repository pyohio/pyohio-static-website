Cliff Jenkins is an accomplished Lead Senior Developer and Coding Instructor with 30+ years of experience in software development and education. Having a proven track record in mentoring diverse teams, enhancing curriculums, and achieving student satisfaction. A polygot developer with expertise in several programming languages and a subject matter expert in agile methodologies.  His primary focus for past 15+ years has been on integrating innovative technologies along with a commitment to fostering inclusive learning environments and driving technical excellence.

Currently a Lead Developer at Los Alamos National Laboratory,  Cliff is the husband of the beautiful Michelle Jenkins.  Father of four.  Grandfather of two precocious granddaughters.  Besides his life long interest in the computing sciences, Cliff is an avid gardener, amateur pit boss, a ham radio operator (KE8JAE), a rugby and football enthusiast, and a Golden Era Hip-Hop head.

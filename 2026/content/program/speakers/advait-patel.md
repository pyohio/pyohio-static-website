Advait Patel is a Senior Cloud Security and Site Reliability Engineer at Broadcom. His work spans Zero Trust architecture, vulnerability management, compliance-aligned controls, and reliability engineering for distributed systems. He is a Google Developer Expert (google cloud and security), Docker Captain, the creator of OWASP DockSec, and founding member of OWASP AIVSS (Artificial Intelligence Vulnerability Scoring System). Advait regularly speaks at SANS CloudSecNext, OWASP Global AppSec US, OWASP SnowFROC, QConAI, PlatformCon, Redgate Summit, CornCon, BlueTeamCon, and many more. He also publishes research work in AI and cloud security and is an author of multiple security books with Springer and Wiley.

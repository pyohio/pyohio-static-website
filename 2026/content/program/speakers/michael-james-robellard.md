Michael Robellard has been part of the Cleveland Python and PyOhio communities since 2007. He's worked at American Greetings, OnShift, EHR Tutor, ATI, and currently ChartFlow — cofounding two of those (EHR Tutor and ChartFlow) and selling one to ATI. He gave his first PyOhio talk in 2014 and is excited to be back on stage after a long hiatus. When he's not building software, he's usually talking about building software.

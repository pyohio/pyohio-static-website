Maker, breaker, and professional eater of bread, Joe Kaufeld likes to try and leave the world a little better than he found it. A senior software engineer by day, Joe has worked in everything from fintech to education. Notable projects include TranscribersOfReddit (crowdsourced accessibility, powered by Python), FilamentColors.xyz (the world's largest 3D printing filament archive), or `asbestos` (a library for testing SnowflakeDB calls).

Carrie is a native Ohio-an with an academic background in STEM (including both Undergraduate and Master's degrees). She has navigated a storied career, pivoting into Data Engineering several years ago. She thrives on the challenge of evolving technologies, complex problem-solving, and the opportunity to work with world-class teams.
With 10 years Python, SQL, and AWS experience across the public and private sectors, Carrie specializes in writing well documented, clean, maintainable code. She is comfortable navigating complex codebases and actively collaborates with peers to ensure high-performance standards. For the past six years, she has sharpened her focus on the data management pillars, focused on data quality.  
When she isn't architecting data pipelines, Carrie balances her bookshelf between technical deep-dives and immersive fantasy novels. After work, you can find her at paint night or on a walk. She is always up for a chat about the daily NYT games—or her rescue cat, Buttercup

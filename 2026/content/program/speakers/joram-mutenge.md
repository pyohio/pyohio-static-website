Joram Mutenge is an independent data consultant at [Conterval](https://www.conterval.com/), where he helps small and mid-sized companies turn raw data into clear business insights and publication-ready visualizations. He is the author of [Deep Analysis With Polars](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GXG1BHG6/) and works extensively with modern data tools in Python. Joram holds a master’s degree in Data Science from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Outside of work, he enjoys cycling, listening to podcasts, and keeping up with his favorite TV series.

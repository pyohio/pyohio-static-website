PyOhio 2026 features a full weekend of Python talks, lightning talks, and community-driven open spaces.

## Talks

Our talk lineup will feature sessions covering a wide range of Python topics, from beginner to advanced. See the [full talk list](/2026/program/talks/) when announced.

## Keynote Speakers

Our [keynote speakers](/2026/program/keynote-speakers/) will be announced closer to the event.

## Lightning Talks

[Lightning Talks](/2026/program/lightning-talks/) are quick 5-minute talks on any topic of interest to the PyOhio community. Anyone can present — sign up at the event!

## Open Spaces

[Open Spaces](/2026/program/open-spaces/) are self-organized meetups in dedicated rooms during the conference. Anyone can participate or host one.

## Schedule

The [full schedule](/2026/program/schedule/) will be published closer to the event.

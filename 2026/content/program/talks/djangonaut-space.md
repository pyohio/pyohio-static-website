Permission to board granted. Discover how Djangonaut Space has empowered people to contribute to Django, its ecosystem, and the broader python universe.

Djangonaut Space is a free, 8-week group mentorship program for aspiring Django contributors. Since its pilot in late 2023, we've run seven sessions with ambitious goals: making Django contributions more sustainable, leveling up contributors, increasing diversity, and cultivating future leaders.

Three years in, the results speak for themselves. And yes, space puns intended.

Have you ever wondered about `float('NaN')`? What it is? What you can do with it? What you shouldn't do with it? 
In this talk we'll take a look at `NaN`, its origin, and its uniqueness. We'll take a look at some code that uses it and have a live demo showing some of it off.
Let's take some time and explore this Not a Number and what it does (and doesn't do) in Python.

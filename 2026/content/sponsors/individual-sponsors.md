Thank you to our generous individual supporters who help make PyOhio free for everyone to attend.

## About Individual Sponsorship

PyOhio is proud to offer free admission to everyone however the event has significant costs. We need corporate sponsors to cover the bulk of these costs however we've had individuals ask how they can contribute.

Details on how to make an individual sponsorship donation for PyOhio 2026 will be announced soon.

**For a $50+ donation, Individual Sponsors get:**

- Their name listed on the sponsors page (optional)
- A limited edition PyOhio Supporter sticker at badge pickup

*Please note:*

- This level is for individuals only.
- Names or nicknames only. We reserve the right to remove company or inappropriate names.
- Donating doesn't automatically include registration. Be sure to also register if you plan on attending PyOhio.
- If your company would like to sponsor PyOhio, please see the [Sponsorship Prospectus](/next/sponsors/prospectus/).

If you'd like to support PyOhio in some other way please email [info@pyohio.org](mailto:info@pyohio.org).

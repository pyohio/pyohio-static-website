# Directions & Parking

The Cleveland State University Student Center is located at **2121 Euclid Avenue, Cleveland, Ohio 44115**.

## Driving

Use the address above for GPS navigation. The Student Center is on the north side of Euclid Avenue on the CSU campus.

## Parking

There are a number of parking options near the venue:

- **Off-campus private lots** — Several private lots are available in the area surrounding the campus.
- **Street parking** — Metered street parking is available in the area. Many meters accept the ParkMobile app.

### CSU Campus Parking

> **Important:** If you park on campus, be sure to follow all posted rules to avoid being ticketed, booted, or towed.

Recommended campus parking:

- **Central Garage** — Pay-on-exit garage on East 21st Street
- **South Garage** — Pay-on-exit garage on East 21st Street

## From Crowne Plaza Hotel

The Crowne Plaza Cleveland at Playhouse Square is approximately a 10-minute / 0.5-mile walk from the Student Center.

## Flying

Cleveland Hopkins International Airport (CLE) is approximately 15–20 minutes from downtown Cleveland by car.

### Public Transit from Airport

Take the RTA Red Line from the airport (~30 minutes) to Tower City Center, then transfer to the HealthLine (bus rapid transit) or walk to the CSU campus.

# Venue

PyOhio 2026 will be held at the **Cleveland State University Student Center**, located at **2121 Euclid Avenue, Cleveland, OH 44115**.

> [!note]
> Please review our [Health & Safety Guidelines](/2026/about/health-and-safety/) before attending.

The conference will take place on the **3rd floor** of the Student Center.

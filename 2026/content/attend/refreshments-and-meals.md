# Refreshments & Meals

PyOhio will provide a limited number of bottles of water during the event. All meals are on your own. There are a number of options within a short walk of the venue.

## Conference Refreshments

A limited number of bottles of water will be available while supplies last. Water fill stations are located near the restrooms, so we suggest bringing your own water bottle!

## Meal Suggestions

There are many places to find food in the area but here are some organizer recommendations:

- **Bombay Chaat** (~2 min walk) — Casual Indian street fare
- **Yum Village** (~0.2 mi / ~4 min walk) — Afro-Caribbean with halal and vegan options. Open Saturday only
- **Sittoo's** (~0.4 mi / ~8 min walk) — Lebanese dine-in and take-out
- **Superior Pho** (~0.8 mi / ~16 min walk) — Vietnamese, great for a quick lunch
- **Ha Ahn** (~0.8 mi / ~16 min walk) — Korean and sushi. Sunday hours vary, call ahead
- **Masthead** (~1 mi / ~18 min walk) — Brewery with pizzas and sandwiches. Great for larger groups because of picnic table seating and individual payment at order time
- **Southern Tier Brewery** (~1 mi / ~18 min walk) — Craft beer and pub fare
- **Pho Thang Cafe** (~1 mi / ~18 min walk) — Vietnamese pho and rice dishes
- **Butcher and the Brewer** (~1 mi / ~20 min walk) — New American brewpub with shared plates
- **LJ Shanghai** (~1 mi / ~20 min walk) — Shanghainese known for soup dumplings. Cash only
- **Cordelia** (~1 mi / ~20 min walk) — Upscale New American. Saturday dinner; Sunday brunch only
- **Mabel's BBQ** (~1.1 mi / ~20 min walk) — Cleveland-style barbecue on E. 4th Street
- **Noble Beast Brewing** (~1.1 mi / ~22 min walk) — Brewpub with pub fare

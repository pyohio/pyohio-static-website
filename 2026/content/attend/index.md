# Attend PyOhio 2026

PyOhio 2026 will be held **Saturday & Sunday, July 25–26, 2026** at the Cleveland State University Student Center in Cleveland, OH.

PyOhio is FREE to attend thanks to the generous support of our sponsors and individual donors.

## Black Python Devs Leadership Summit

PyOhio is co-hosting the [Black Python Devs Leadership Summit](/2026/program/black-python-devs-leadership-summit/) on Friday, July 24, 2026, the day before PyOhio at the same venue. The summit requires a separate (also free) ticket, which you can add alongside your PyOhio registration.

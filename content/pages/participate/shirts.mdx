# T-Shirt Fundraiser

This year we are using our t-shirt sale fundraiser to support our efforts
to reduce the cost of attending the conference for speakers at future
PyOhio events.
All of the proceeds from t-shirt sales this year will go toward those
costs for the next in-person PyOhio event (planned for 2023.)
